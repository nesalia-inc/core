## The 3 Uncomfortable Truths

### Truth 1: The Market Window Is Closing

The fp-ts/Effect merger uncertainty won't last forever. neverthrow will fix React 19 eventually. We have 6-12 months to establish @deessejs/fp before the market re-consolidates.

**Implication:** Speed matters more than perfection.

### Truth 2: Most Developers Won't Switch

The vast majority of TypeScript developers use try/catch and won't change. We should focus on the already-converted (neverthrow users, fp-ts users) rather than fighting the try/catch incumbency.

**Implication:** Target the right audience, not everyone.

### Truth 3: Enterprise Takes Time

CTOs don't adopt libraries based on blog posts. They need testimonials, benchmarks, compliance docs, and proof of production usage. These take time to build.

**Implication:** Developer adoption first (0-6 months), enterprise later (6-18 months).
